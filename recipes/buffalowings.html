Buffalo Wings

Got a game coming up? Family gathering? Well, warm up your oven, because these scrumptious baked buffalo wings will spice up your party!

Don't worry about a fryer, a grill, or even an airfryer, as these delicious wings can come straight from your oven. You can add whatever sides or sauces you like, without having to go through the hassle of ordering from someone else!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Chicken Wings
  • Butter
  • Hot Sauce
  • Cooking Spray

Steps

  1. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil, and grease with cooking spray.
  2. Place all dry ingredients (flour, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, and salt) into a resealable plastic bag, and shake to mix. Add the chicken wings to the bag and toss until well coated.
  3. Move wings from bag to a prepared baking sheet and refrigerate for at least an hour.
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  5. While the oven is preheating, whisk together melted butter and hot sauce in a small bowl. Dip wings into mixture, and place back onto the baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Flip wings and continue to cook until crispy and no longer pink in the center, about 25 more minutes.
